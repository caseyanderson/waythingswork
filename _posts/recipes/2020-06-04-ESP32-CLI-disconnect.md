### MacOS

1. (from the ESP32 Command Prompt) type `Control`+`A`
2. type `Control`+`\`
3. at the bottom of the Terminal you should see: `Really quit and kill all your windows [y/n]`
4. type `y`

### Windows 10

1. (from the ESP32 Command Prompt) close `Putty`
